  • 26th December, 2003

    Bam Earthquake 2003

    We have been alert that Kerman province of southeastern Iran was hit by earthquake. The shock had a moment magnitude of 6.6 and a maximum Mecalli intensity of Violent. The earthquake was particularly destructive in Bam, with the death toll amounting to at least 26,271 people. So the earthquake are called 2003 Bam Earthquake. We dispatch our team to help Iran at the time.

  • 16th December, 2019

    Coronavirus (Covid-19)

    The COVID-19 pandemic, also known as the coronavirus pandemic, is an ongoing global pandemic of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The virus was first identified in December 2019 in Wuhan, China. The World Health Organization declared a Public Health Emergency of International Concern regarding COVID-19 on 30 January 2020, and later declared a pandemic on 11 March 2020. As of 16 May 2021, more than 162 million cases have been confirmed, with more than 3.37 million deaths attributed to COVID-19, making it one of the deadliest pandemics in history.
